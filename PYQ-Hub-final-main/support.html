Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload PYQs to earn rewards?
Simply click on "Upload & Earn" in the dashboard, fill in the required details including your college name, and upload your PYQ files. You'll earn points for each successful upload!
What types of files can I upload?
We accept PDF, DOC, DOCX, and image files (JPG, PNG) containing previous year question papers. Make sure the files are clear and readable.
How does the advanced search work?
Use the advanced search feature to find specific topics, subjects, or question types. You can filter by college, year, subject, and keywords to find exactly what you need.
Can I manually enter my college name?
Yes! In the upload form, you can either select from the dropdown options or manually type your college name in the college field. We've added specific options for Manav Rachna institutions.
How do I join the community discussions?
Click on "Community" in the navigation to access our discussion forums. You can ask questions, share study tips, and connect with other students.
What are the rewards for uploading PYQs?
You earn points for each upload, which can be redeemed for various benefits. The more quality content you contribute, the more rewards you can unlock!
Is my data secure?
Absolutely! We use Firebase for secure authentication and data storage, and Cloudinary for safe image storage. Your personal information is protected.
How can I get help with technical issues?
For technical support, you can email us at support.abc@gmail.com or call us at 9876543210. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.
